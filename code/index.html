Floodplain restoration can transform landscapes from greenhouse gas emitters into carbon sinks

Pioneering research by WWF shows periodical flooding enhances biomass production, increases carbon sequestration, and reduces overall greenhouse gas emissions / February, 03 2025

In celebration of World Wetlands Day, WWF Central and Eastern Europe released new research revealing the critical role of floodplain restoration in combating climate change.

The findings show that restoring former floodplains could transform landscapes from greenhouse gas emitters into carbon sinks.
